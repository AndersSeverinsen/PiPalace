Clap Light Switch

The Clapper! A Rasberry Pi detects a double clap, and sends a web request, controlling the Hue lights, thought IFTTT.

Introduction

Components:

- A Raspberry Pi Zero W

- LED Strip

- Screws, washers, bolts and standoffs.

- Soldering iron and solder

- 4mm plywood

- 3mm white acrylic
